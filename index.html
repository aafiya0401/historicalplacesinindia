Introduction

	

Indian History is full of the rise and fall of many kingdoms and empires. 
Monuments, built by the kings and they perform of every period throw light on the past history of India. 
these monuments exhibit the glory of India and are part of our cultural heritage. 
Almost all states of India boast of some or the other important historical monuments. 
Thousands of tourists visit India to have a glimpse of its important historical places.
