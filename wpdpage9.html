Travelling tips for Taj Mahal - a Tourist Guide

Planning a trip to India? Then the Taj Mahal is definitely on your list of must-visit attractions, right? But before you embark on your journey, here are some essential tips that you must know to make the most of your visit to this beautiful white mausoleum.

  • Know the best time to visit - from March to June

  • Know what your ticket includes

  • Tajmahal is closed on fridays, so do not make any plan for this day

  • If you are going by road, then take only express way. that is the fastest route.

  • Always remember that vehicles are not allowed upto 1 km from TAJ.

  • There are different queues for indians and foreigners.
